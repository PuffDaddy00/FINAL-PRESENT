Simplify Sustainable Computing for Education

Empower public schools with secure, efficient, and environmentally responsible computer recycling solutions. At E-Recycle, we transform electronic waste into educational tools—minimizing environmental impact while maximizing student access to technology.

About Us

At E-Recycle, we are driven by a simple mission: to give unused computer components a second life and provide students with the technology they need to succeed. By recycling, reassembling, and donating computers to public schools, we bridge the gap between sustainability and education.

We believe every piece of technology has value—not just for the environment, but for the future of learning. Instead of letting electronic waste harm our planet, we transform it into opportunities for students, equipping classrooms with the tools to explore, create, and grow.

How We Help Schools

Component Collection & Assembly

We gather unused and surplus computer parts, validate their functionality, and assemble them into fully operational systems.

Donations to Public Schools

Each refurbished computer is donated to a public school in need—supporting underserved students and equitable access to technology.

Verified Process & Support

Our process is transparent, documented, and backed by reliable logistics and expert support at every step.
