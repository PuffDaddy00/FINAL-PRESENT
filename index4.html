From E-Waste to Education

Davao City - Thirty-three refurbished desktop computers have been turned over to Davao City High School this week through the "From E-waste to Education" project, an initiative that transforms unused electronics into valuable learning tools for students.

Repurposing for a Better Future

Our program transforms discarded electronics into valuable resources, creating a circular economy that benefits both the environment and our youth. We give old gadgets a second life by turning them into learning tools for schools and communities in need.
